Boy band BTS’s two-day online concert and meet-and-greet “BTS 2021 Muster Sowoozoo” was viewed by approximately 1.33 million fans all over the world, according to Big Hit Music on Tuesday. The concert was held on June 13 and 14 in celebration of BTS’s eighth debut anniversary. The 1.33 million viewers were from 195 countries, the agency said. ARMY, or BTS fans, use the term “muster” to refer to large fan meet-and-greets. “BTS 2021 Muster Sowoozoo” was BTS’s first meet-and-greet since 2019, albeit online this year. The online concert featured performances of BTS’s megahits “Dynamite” (2020), "Butter” (2021) and more.
